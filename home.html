COLLEGE BUS TRACKING SYSTEM

College bus Tracking System (VTS) is the technology used to determin location of a vehicle using different methods like GPS and other radio navigation system operating through satellites and ground based stations. By following triangulation or trilateration methods the tracking system enables to calculate easy and accurate locati of the vehicle. Vehicle information like location details, speed, distance traveled etc be viewed on a digital mapping with the help of a software via Internet. Even data can bstored and downloaded to a computer from the GPS unit at a base station and that later be used for analysis. This system is an important tool for tracking each vehicle a given period of time and now it is becoming increasingly popular for people havingexpensive cars and hence as a theft prevention and retrieval device. i. The system consists of modern hardware and software components enabling one to track their vehicle online or offline. Any vehicle tracking system consists of mainly three parts mobile vehicle unit, fixed based station and, database and software system.
